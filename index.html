Red Rising Saga

Series Description

The Red Rising Saga is a dystopian science-fiction series consisting of two trilogies written by author Pierce Brown. The first trilogy includes Red Rising, Golden Son, and Morning Star, published in 2014, 2015, and 2016, respectively. The second trilogy includes Iron Gold, published in 2017, and Dark Age, released in 2019. The final book is currently being written and the publication date is unknown.
